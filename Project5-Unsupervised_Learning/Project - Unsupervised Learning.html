#we range the clusters from 2 to 10
clusters=range(2,10)
#average distances between the points on the cluster to itself and the others.
meanDistortions=[]

for k in clusters:
    model=KMeans(n_clusters=k)
    model.fit(df_model)
    prediction=model.predict(df_model)
    sc = silhouette_score(df_model, prediction)
    print("Silhoutte_score for clusters {} = {}".format(k, sc))
    meanDistortions.append(model.inertia_)
    
    
plt.plot(clusters, meanDistortions, 'bx-')
plt.xlabel('k')
plt.ylabel('Average distortion')
plt.title('Selecting k with the Elbow Method')

Obseration on KMeans Clustering¶

Table of Contents

  1. There are 4 segmentation of the customers.

  2. Segment 1 :

    • Least credit limit
    • Holds least amount of credit cards
    • Does not visit bank often
    • Has second highest number of visits online.
    • Has the most number of calls made.

    Segment 2 :

    • Second highest Credit limit
    • Second highest number of credit cards held
    • Visits bank often
    • Does not visit online
    • Second highest number of calls made.

    Segment 3 :

    • Highest Credit limit
    • Maximum amount of credit cards
    • Very less bank visit
    • Highest online visit
    • least amount of calls made.

    Segment 4 :

    • Third Highest Credit limit
    • Third Maximum amount of credit cards
    • Highest bank visit

Recommendations based on KMeans Clustering¶

Table of Contents

  1. The highest credit holding customers are tech savvy, we must improve in our online support experience.
  2. The second highest credit holding customers visit bank and make calls for support. We must provide them with best instore experience.
  3. The customers who make the highest number of calls should be encouraged to use the online support experience. They could also be targetted for selling more credit cards through online experience.
